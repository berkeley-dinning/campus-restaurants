Xúm's

  • Previous
  • Next

 

Check for hours

Located near the vibrant MLK building, Xúm's offers a vibrant, health-conscious breakfast and lunch menu. Dine in the cozy indoor setting or step outside to eat. For breakfast, try delicious options like a breakfast burrito, plant-based burrito, or egg sandwich. For lunch, order from a world-class menu including daily special salads and soups, and add a side of fries or rice bowl. The diverse, talented team is proud to serve around 900 students every day.

Recommendation: Specialty salad
